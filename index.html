Intermediate

Intermediate education (Grades 11-12) acts as a stepping stone between school and higher education. It helps students build a strong foundation for their academic and career goals. Choose from Science, Commerce, or Arts, depending on your interests and aspirations.

Degree

Undergraduate degrees are your first step into specialized higher education after completing Intermediate. Explore courses like B.A., B.Sc., B.Com., B.Tech., and more, tailored to your interests and career plans.
